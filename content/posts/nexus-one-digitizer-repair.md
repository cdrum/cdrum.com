As some of you may know, I got the Nexus One a few weeks ago. After having it about 3 weeks, I dropped it. The glass digitizer cracked. I sourced a replacement digitizer (glass screen) via Alibaba from China. I had to buy bulk (10) - so anyone in Singapore who has a broken Nexus One, email me. I'm selling. I fixed it, took about 2 hours, but it works! No left over screws! Thanks to iFixIt at [http://www.ifixit.com/Teardown/Nexus-One/1654/1](http://www.ifixit.com/Teardown/Nexus-One/1654/1)!
